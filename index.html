Job opportunities

The European Centre for the Development of Vocational Training (Cedefop) is an EU agency located in Thessaloniki, Greece with approximately 120 staff and Seconded national experts.

We offer an intellectually stimulating, dynamic multicultural environment. We are an equal opportunities employer, we are committed to inclusion and celebrate diversity.

Image

As an agency of the European Union, we abide by the Staff Regulations of Officials of the European Communities and Conditions of employment of other servants of the European Communities.

We offer competitive remuneration package, excellent opportunities for professional development and arrangements that help staff balance their work and private lives.

All Cedefop employment opportunities are announced on our website. We look for highly well-qualified candidates with high degree of professionalism and ethical standards.

Our selection procedures are designed to guarantee transparency and equal treatment for all candidates.

In our Guide for applicants, you may find practical information to guide you through the selection process.
